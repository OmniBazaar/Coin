Notes for Auditors
--------------------------------------------------------------------

- 15 of the top 20 are UUPS-upgradeable. Verify that upgrade
  authority flows through OmniGovernance -> OmniTimelockController
  with proper delays. Any contract where admin can upgrade without
  timelock is a critical finding.

- The governance chain is:
  OmniGovernance (propose) -> OmniTimelockController (48h/7d delay)
  -> target contract. EmergencyGuardian provides fast-path pause.

- Three contracts use plain Ownable instead of Ownable2Step:
  LegacyBalanceClaim (#20), OmniBonding (#23), OmniPaymaster.
  Single-key compromise risk is elevated for these.

- COTI V2 MPC contracts (PrivateOmniCoin, PrivateDEX,
  PrivateDEXSettlement, PrivateWBTC/WETH/USDC) depend on the MPC
  precompile at 0x...0064. Auditors should verify behavior when
  MPC is unavailable or returns unexpected values.

- Total Solidity across all 54 production contracts: ~55,000 lines.
  The top 20 alone account for ~23,000 lines.
